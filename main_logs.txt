Day 8
Implemented GPU inference pipeline using Google Colab (Tesla T4). Compared CPU ONNX Runtime, ONNX Runtime CUDA, and TensorRT FP16. Moved preprocessing to GPU (resize and normalization), built a TensorRT engine from the ONNX model, and benchmarked the complete pipeline.

Results:
- CPU: 937.98 ms (1.07 FPS)
- ONNX CUDA: 31.32 ms (31.93 FPS)
- TensorRT FP16: 27.93 ms (35.80 FPS)
